A brief description of Dali-Tech International

-Dali-Tech International is a decentralised group of computer experts
-It was found by Preston Khumbula while he was an I.T student at Mutare Polytechnic and decided to take the expertise to the world at an affordable price
-Actually Dali-Tech started as a non profit making organisation that aimed at helping the non computing communities get help and knowledge on how to solve their own daily computer realted problems
-It later transformed into a business venture but also aiming at making I.T solutions available at low cost to those in need
-Dali-Tech International today have a lot of clients from and beyond Zimbabwe
